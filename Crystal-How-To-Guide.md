## Getting Started

### What is Crystal?
Crystal is a comprehensive video recording and sharing platform similar to Loom, designed for teams and individuals who need to create, share, and collaborate on video content. The platform offers both web-based and desktop applications with advanced AI-powered features.

### Key Features
- **Screen Recording**: Record your screen with audio and webcam
- **Team Collaboration**: Create workspaces and invite team members
- **AI-Powered**: Automatic transcription, summaries, and AI tools
- **Multiple Sharing Options**: Direct links, embedded videos, and rich previews
- **Desktop App**: Native desktop application for seamless recording
- **Resolution Options**: HD (1080p) and SD (720p) recording quality

## Creating Workspaces

### Personal Workspace
When you first sign up for Crystal, a personal workspace is automatically created for you:
- **Name**: `[Your Name]'s Workspace`
- **Type**: Personal workspace
- **Access**: Private to you only

### Creating Additional Workspaces
**Note**: Creating additional workspaces requires a PRO subscription.

#### Steps to Create a New Workspace:
1. **Navigate to Workspace Settings**
   - Click on the workspace dropdown in the sidebar
   - Select "Create New Workspace"

2. **Enter Workspace Details**
   - Provide a descriptive name for your workspace
   - Choose workspace type (Public/Private)

3. **Workspace Types**
   - **Personal**: Private workspace for individual use
   - **Public**: Team workspace for collaboration (PRO feature)

4. **Workspace Management**
   - Rename workspaces anytime
   - Switch between workspaces from the sidebar
   - Delete workspaces (with proper permissions)

### Workspace Features
- **Video Organization**: All videos are organized within workspaces
- **Folder System**: Create folders to organize videos
- **Member Management**: Add team members to collaborate
- **Access Control**: Control who can view and edit content

## Adding Team Members

### Inviting Team Members
**Note**: Team collaboration features require a PRO subscription.

#### Steps to Invite Members:
1. **Access Workspace Settings**
   - Navigate to your workspace
   - Click on workspace settings or member management

2. **Send Invitations**
   - Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
   - Add a personalized message (optional)
   - Send the invitation

3. **Invitation Process**
   - Invited members receive an email notification
   - They can accept or decline the invitation
   - Once accepted, they gain access to the workspace

### Member Permissions
- **View Access**: Members can view all videos in the workspace
- **Comment Access**: Members can add comments and feedback
- **Upload Access**: Members can upload and share videos
- **Admin Access**: Workspace creators have full administrative control

### Managing Team Members
- **View Member List**: See all current workspace members
- **Remove Members**: Remove members from the workspace
- **Resend Invitations**: Resend invitations to pending members
- **Member Activity**: Track member activity and contributions

## Recording Videos

### Desktop App Recording

#### Installation
1. **Download the Desktop App**
   - Visit the Crystal website
   - Download the desktop application for your operating system
   - Install the application

2. **Initial Setup**
   - Launch the desktop app
   - Sign in with your Crystal account
   - Grant necessary permissions for screen recording

#### Recording Process

##### 1. Configure Recording Settings
- **Screen Selection**: Choose which screen or application to record
- **Audio Input**: Select microphone or audio input device
- **Camera**: Choose webcam for picture-in-picture recording
- **Resolution**: Select HD (1080p) or SD (720p) quality
  - **HD (1080p)**: Available for PRO users only
  - **SD (720p)**: Available for all users

##### 2. Start Recording
- **Recording Controls**: Use the floating recording toolbar
- **Start/Stop**: Click the record button to start/stop recording
- **Pause**: Pause recording without stopping
- **Timer**: View recording duration in real-time
- **Preview**: Toggle preview mode during recording

##### 3. Recording Features
- **Screen Capture**: Record entire screen or specific applications
- **Audio Recording**: Capture system audio and microphone
- **Webcam Overlay**: Include your face in the recording
- **Real-time Preview**: See what you're recording as you go
- **Automatic Processing**: Videos are automatically processed and uploaded

### Web-Based Recording
- **Browser Recording**: Record directly from your web browser
- **Limited Features**: Basic recording without advanced controls
- **Quick Access**: No installation required

### Recording Best Practices
1. **Test Your Setup**: Always test audio and video before recording
2. **Choose Appropriate Quality**: Use HD for presentations, SD for quick messages
3. **Prepare Your Environment**: Ensure good lighting and minimal distractions
4. **Plan Your Content**: Outline what you want to cover before recording
5. **Keep It Concise**: Shorter videos are more engaging and easier to process

## Desktop App Installation

### System Requirements
- **Operating System**: Windows, macOS, or Linux
- **Memory**: Minimum 4GB RAM (8GB recommended)
- **Storage**: 500MB free space for the application
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection for uploads

### Installation Steps
1. **Download the Application**
   - Visit the official Crystal website
   - Click "Download Desktop App"
   - Choose your operating system

2. **Install the Application**
   - Run the installer
   - Follow the installation wizard
   - Grant necessary permissions

3. **First Launch**
   - Sign in with your Crystal account
   - Configure recording settings
   - Test your microphone and camera

### Desktop App Features
- **Floating Toolbar**: Always-accessible recording controls
- **System Integration**: Deep integration with your operating system
- **Background Recording**: Record even when the app is minimized
- **Quick Upload**: Automatic upload to your Crystal workspace
- **Offline Recording**: Record without internet, upload when connected

### Troubleshooting Desktop App
- **Permission Issues**: Ensure the app has screen recording permissions
- **Audio Problems**: Check microphone permissions and settings
- **Upload Issues**: Verify internet connection and account status
- **Performance**: Close unnecessary applications during recording
